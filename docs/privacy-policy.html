Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Color Match Spin Rush handles information.

Information We Collect

Color Match Spin Rush does not require account registration and does not directly ask users to provide personal information.

Advertising

Color Match Spin Rush uses Google AdMob to show optional rewarded ads. Users may choose to watch a rewarded ad to receive an extra life in the game.

Google AdMob may collect and process certain information such as device identifiers, advertising data, app interactions, diagnostics, and approximate location data according to Google's own policies.

Game Data

The app may store simple game-related data locally on the device, such as best score, sound settings, vibration settings, and lives status. This data is used only to provide the game experience.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob. You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services here: Google Partner Sites Policy.

Children's Privacy

Color Match Spin Rush is not specifically directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
